What is this?

Video Compressor is a 100% client-side video compression tool. Your files never leave your browser — no uploads, no servers, no privacy concerns. It uses WebCodecs (GPU-accelerated, Chrome/Edge) or FFmpeg.wasm (universal fallback) depending on your browser.

How to use

  1. 1
    Select a video

    Drag & drop a file onto the upload area, or click to pick one. Supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV and more (up to 2 GB).

  2. 2
    Adjust options

    Set the Quality (CRF) — lower value = better quality, larger file. Choose a target resolution, and optionally strip the audio track.

  3. 3
    Compress

    Click Compress and watch the real-time progress bar. The engine label shows whether GPU (WebCodecs) or software (FFmpeg) is being used.

  4. 4
    Download

    Once done, the before/after sizes are shown. Click Download to save the compressed file.

Tips

  • CRF 18–22 — High quality, modest size reduction
  • CRF 23–28 — Balanced quality and compression (recommended)
  • CRF 29–40 — Maximum compression, noticeable quality loss
  • Dropping to 720p often cuts file size in half with little visible difference on most screens.
  • Removing audio is great for silent clips — it can save 5–15% extra.
